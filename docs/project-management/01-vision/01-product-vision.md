## Vision Statement

> **For anyone sharing life with others** — partners, family, roommates, friends — who struggle to keep shared expenses, tasks, meals, and errands organized across separate apps, **LIFEY** is **your life together, simplified**: a unified hub for expenses, to-do lists, recipes, and groceries, powered by an AI agent you can talk to. Unlike generic tools built for individuals, LIFEY **understands you're a team** and connects every part of your shared life.

---

## Breakdown

| Component | LIFEY |
|-----------|-------|
| **Target audience** | Anyone sharing life together — partners, family, roommates, friends (starting with personal use) |
| **Problem** | Shared life is scattered across separate apps. Nothing handles the "yours, mine, and ours" split. Nothing connects a recipe to a grocery list to a shared expense. |
| **Solution** | Mobile app (iOS + Android) with four integrated modules — Expenses, To-Do, Recipes, Groceries — surfaced through a conversational AI agent |
| **Value proposition** | One place for your shared life. Talk to the AI, and it handles the rest. |
| **Key differentiation** | Built for *groups who share life*, with a **chat-based AI agent** that accesses every module, plus the ability to **create your own agents** |

## Tagline

> **LIFEY — your life together, simplified.**

---

## Guiding Principles

| Principle | What it means for LIFEY |
|-----------|------------------------|
| **Shared-first** | Every feature assumes >1 person. Personal vs. shared is a core concept, not an afterthought. |
| **AI-native** | The AI agent isn't a bolton — it's woven into every module. You can talk to your life. |
| **Agent-extensible** | Users can create their own agents, not just use the default one. |
| **Private by default** | Personal data stays personal. Sharing is explicit, never accidental. |

---

## Non-Vision (What LIFEY is not)

- ❌ A generic finance app
- ❌ A standalone recipe manager
- ❌ A social network
- ❌ A project management tool for work
